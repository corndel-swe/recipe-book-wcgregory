Pizza

Ingredients:
Pizza base
  • 300g Bread Flour
  • 15g Butter
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Yeast
Topping
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Mushrooms
  • Pepper
  • ...
Steps:
  1. Mix the base ingredients together
  2. Need and put aside and allow to rise
  3. Apply toppings
  4. Cook in the oven
